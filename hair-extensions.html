Hair Extensions & Braids

Professional extension and braiding services to add length, volume, and style to your hair. We offer a variety of methods to suit your lifestyle and hair goals.

Sew-In Extensions

Sew-In Extensions

$275+

Also called a weave. This service includes a wash blow dry braids. Sew-in cut and simple style. We do not include hair in the price. Feel free to bring in your own hair. Otherwise, we can arrange to order hair for you through our preferred supplier. Please book appointments early as it takes time for the hair to ship to us since this service takes several hours. We recommend booking in advance.

Crochet Braids

Crochet Braids

$175+

Another great protective style to maintain your hair while rocking some fun hair on top! Generally it's more common to use fuller natural looking textures, but you can chat with your stylist on options to get the look you want! Partial Crochet prices will vary.

Twists or Locs

Twists or Locs

$180+

Includes Havana, Marley, or Kinky Twists (medium or large) and Faux or Goddess Locs! We do not provide the hair for these services, but we love to help provide a recommendation since these services take several hours. It's best to schedule in advance.

Braid Styles

Braid Styles

$75+

Pricing depends on the complexity of the style desired in the condition and length of your hair. Chat with the Braider to book your appointment!

Box Braids

Box Braids

$140+

This is a great protective style with iconic box or square shape parts/dividers for the braids. This style generally includes hair for length and thickness. Chat with one of our Braiders for more details, as this is a long service, it's best to book in advance.

Important Notes

  • We do not provide hair for most services - please bring your own or we can help arrange ordering
  • Extension and braiding services take several hours - please book in advance
  • Pricing varies based on hair length, condition, and style complexity
  • Consultation recommended for first-time clients
  • Maintenance appointments available for ongoing care
